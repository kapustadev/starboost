When it comes to building a rock-solid online reputation, you shouldn't put all your eggs in one basket. While Google Maps is often the first place customers look, ignoring other platforms leaves money on the table. 

At StarsBoost, we specialize in providing high-quality, authentic reviews across the **Big Three** platforms: Google, Facebook, and Trustpilot. Here is why you need all three.

## 1. Google Maps: The Local SEO King

Google Maps is the undisputed champion of local search. When someone types "best restaurant near me" or "plumber in London", Google’s algorithm heavily favors businesses with a high volume of positive, consistent reviews.

**Why it matters:**
- Direct impact on local SEO (the "Local Pack").
- High conversion rate: Customers often call or get directions directly from the search results.

## 2. Facebook: Social Proof & Word of Mouth

Facebook reviews (now called "Recommendations") are incredibly powerful for social proof. When a user sees that their friends or community members recommend your business, trust is established instantly.

**Why it matters:**
- Highly visible to people browsing social media.
- Enhances your Facebook Ads performance.
- Great for B2C businesses (retail, beauty, fitness).

## 3. Trustpilot: The Gold Standard for E-Commerce & B2B

If you run an online store, a SaaS company, or a B2B service, Trustpilot is the badge of honor your website needs. Consumers actively look for the green Trustpilot stars before pulling out their credit cards.

**Why it matters:**
- Trustpilot widgets on your checkout page can increase conversion rates by up to 30%.
- Trustpilot reviews often appear in Google search results with rich snippets (star ratings under your website link).

## The Winning Strategy

Don't just buy 100 Google reviews and call it a day. A natural, trustworthy business has a presence everywhere. We recommend a balanced approach:
- **60%** of your review acquisition budget on Google Maps
- **20%** on Facebook
- **20%** on Trustpilot
