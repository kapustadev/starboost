Many business owners believe that Local SEO is simply a numbers game: whoever has the most reviews wins the top spot in the Google Local Pack. While total review volume is important, the 2026 Google algorithm has shifted its focus heavily toward a metric called **Review Velocity**.

## What is Review Velocity?

Review Velocity refers to the rate and consistency at which your business acquires new reviews. 

Imagine two competing coffee shops:
- **Shop A** buys 100 fake bot reviews on a Monday, and then gets zero reviews for the rest of the year.
- **Shop B** consistently earns 2 to 3 organic-looking reviews every single week for a year.

Google's algorithm will heavily penalize Shop A. A sudden, massive spike in reviews followed by absolute silence is the signature footprint of spam. Google will likely filter or delete those reviews, and potentially suspend Shop A's Business Profile entirely. 

Shop B, however, demonstrates healthy, consistent customer engagement. Google rewards this natural Review Velocity with higher rankings and greater visibility.

## The Danger of Cheap Bot Farms

When you buy reviews from cheap providers on Fiverr or shady forums, they use automated bot scripts to deliver all your reviews instantly. They do not understand or care about your Review Velocity. They are actively harming your Local SEO.
