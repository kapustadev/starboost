## What is the Reseller Program?

Our Reseller Portal is designed specifically for bulk buyers who manage multiple businesses or clients. Once your account is upgraded to a Reseller, you gain access to a specialized dashboard tailored for high-volume orders.

## Key Benefits

### 1. Automatic 20% Bulk Discount
As a reseller, you get an automatic **20% discount** on all orders, regardless of the size. This allows you to mark up the price and sell the reviews to your clients at retail value, keeping the difference as pure profit.

### 2. Bulk Ordering Interface
Instead of creating 10 separate orders for 10 different clients, our Reseller Dashboard lets you paste a list of URLs, select the platform and quantity, and checkout in one click. We handle the distribution in the backend.

### 3. White-Label Reporting
When delivering results to your clients, you don't want them knowing you used StarsBoost. Our white-label reporting feature generates a clean, unbranded tracking link showing the progress of the reviews (from "Pending" to "Delivered"). You can safely share this link with your clients.
