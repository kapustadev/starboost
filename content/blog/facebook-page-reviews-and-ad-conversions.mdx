With billions of active users, Facebook remains one of the most powerful platforms for local businesses, e-commerce brands, and service providers. But just having a page isn't enough anymore. 

When a potential customer clicks on your Facebook ad or finds your page organically, the first thing they look at is your **Reviews and Recommendations**.

## The Impact on Facebook Ads

If you are running Facebook or Instagram Ads, your Page's review score directly impacts your Return on Ad Spend (ROAS). 

When users see a sponsored post, they often click on the profile before clicking the link. If your page has a 2-star rating or no reviews at all, the trust is instantly broken. They will bounce. On the other hand, a page loaded with glowing 5-star recommendations acts as the ultimate conversion catalyst.

Furthermore, Facebook's ad algorithm actually penalizes pages with low feedback scores, resulting in higher CPMs (Cost Per Mille) and lower reach.

## Building Community Trust

Facebook is a social platform, which means social proof carries immense weight. When users see that other people in their community highly recommend your business, they are far more likely to engage with your content, share your posts, and ultimately buy your products.
