## The Anatomy of a Dangerous Bot Farm

If you are paying $10 for 50 reviews, you are buying from a bot farm. These providers operate using:
- **Shared Proxies:** Thousands of accounts logging in from the same cheap IP addresses.
- **Fresh Accounts:** Accounts created yesterday with no history, no profile pictures, and bizarre usernames.
- **Instant Delivery:** Blasting all 50 reviews onto your profile within a few hours.

The algorithms at Google and Trustpilot are incredibly sophisticated. They can spot a bot farm instantly. Using these services is a guaranteed way to get your business suspended.
