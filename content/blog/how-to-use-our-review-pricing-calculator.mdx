Transparency is at the core of what we do at StarsBoost. Unlike other agencies that hide their pricing behind "Contact Us" forms, we built a **Dynamic Pricing Calculator** right on our homepage so you know exactly what you're paying for.

Here is a quick guide on how to use it to customize your campaign.

## Step 1: Select Your Platform
The first step is choosing where you want your reviews. We currently support:
- **Google Maps**: The best choice for local businesses.
- **Facebook**: Great for social proof.
- **Trustpilot**: Essential for e-commerce and SaaS.

*Pro tip: The base price varies slightly depending on the platform's security algorithms.*

## Step 2: Choose the Quantity
Select from our preset packages (e.g., 10, 25, 50) or type a **custom amount** into the input field. Need exactly 17 reviews? No problem. The calculator scales perfectly.

## Step 3: Select the Geo-Targeting (Country)
This is where StarsBoost shines. We provide real accounts from **22 different countries**. 
Select the country where your business operates. The price per review adjusts automatically because accounts in certain regions (like the USA or UK) are harder to source than others.

## Step 4: Choose the Text Option
You have three choices for the review text:
1. **No text (rating only)**: Just a 5-star rating. This is the cheapest option but looks less authentic if done in bulk.
2. **Reviewer writes their own text**: Our reviewers will write a natural, organic review based on your business category. This is the **most popular** and recommended option.
3. **We write the text for you (+$2/review)**: If you have specific keywords you want to rank for (e.g., "best vegan pizza in New York"), we will craft the exact text.

## Step 5: Set Delivery Frequency
To make the reviews look 100% organic to Google's algorithms, you should spread them out. The calculator lets you choose delivery speeds ranging from **1 per day** to **1 per week**. 

## Instant Order Summary
As you tweak these options, the **Order Summary** on the right side of the screen updates in real-time. No hidden fees. No surprises.
